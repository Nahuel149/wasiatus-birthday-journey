# Personal Media Inbox

This is the easiest place to give the project unsorted personal material. Files added inside the category folders are ignored by Git, so camera originals and rough writing are not uploaded during deployment.

## Drop folders

- `photos/` — original JPG, JPEG, PNG, HEIC, WebP, or AVIF photographs
- `videos/` — original MP4, MOV, M4V, or WebM clips
- `music/` — MP3, M4A, AAC, OGG, or WAV files you have permission to publish
- `captions-transcripts/` — VTT captions or plain-text transcripts for video/audio
- `writing/` — the final letter, chapter stories, dates, locations, song notes, and love reasons in TXT, Markdown, or Word documents
- `extras/` — Rayden’s drawing, scanned notes, voice messages, finale artwork, or other keepsakes

You do not need to rename or organize files before adding them here. Descriptive filenames such as `wedding-ceremony-01.jpg` or `rayden-first-laugh.mov` are helpful, but optional.

After the files are added, they will be reviewed, renamed to stable memory IDs, optimized, and copied into the app’s publishable folders. Only those reviewed derivatives are deployed.

## Important publication note

The final website is public and unlisted rather than access-controlled. Do not add anything you would be uncomfortable with a visitor saving or forwarding. Public visibility also does not grant music-distribution rights; only add locally hosted music you are allowed to publish.
